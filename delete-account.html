Delete Your Account

You can delete your Notified account directly inside the app. Deleting your account permanently removes your profile and all associated data from our servers.

  1. Open the Notified app on your device.
  2. Tap the menu icon (☰) to open the navigation drawer.
  3. Tap Settings.
  4. Scroll down to the Delete account section.
  5. Tap Delete my account and confirm when prompted.
Note: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data, preferences, and subscription history will be removed immediately.
